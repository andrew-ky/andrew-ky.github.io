On March 19, 2020, West Ottawa Public Schools was notified they would not be returning the next day due to the rise of the COVID-19 pandemic. As days turned to weeks turned to months, the West Ottawa community was left with uncertainty. As a project for my high school Advanced Broadcasting class, here is a short documentary on COVID 19 in the West Ottawa community.
