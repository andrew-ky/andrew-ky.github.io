Senior Survivor is a high school reality competition where 12 seniors live at school for a week, competing in challenges to raise money for When in Need West Ottawa (WINWO), a local community support organization.


Challenges and Solutions: Managing both the fundraising logistics and the production of daily Survivor recap episodes was demanding. I implemented a structured workflow and delegated editing responsibilities to streamline execution. Staying at school late to edit while managing event operations led to burnout. Learning to trust my team and distribute tasks allowed for better time management and event efficiency.
