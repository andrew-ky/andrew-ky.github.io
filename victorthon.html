VictorThon is a 24-hour dance marathon held annually by the University of Michigan's Dance Marathon at the University of Michigan (DMUM). The event is the culmination of our hard work and dedication throughout the year, celebrating how much we fundraised for C.S Mott Children's Hospital. We stand for 24 hours as a symbol of support for our Little Victors.
